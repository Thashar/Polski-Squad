# Privacy Policy — Ender's Echo

**Last updated: 11 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains what data the **Ender's Echo** Discord application ("the bot") collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with. By adding the bot to a server or interacting with it, you agree to this policy.

## 1. Data we collect and store

The bot stores only the minimum data needed to run game leaderboards:

- **Discord user ID and username** — to identify whose score belongs to whom on the leaderboard.
- **Game scores** — the boss name, score value, and timestamp extracted from screenshots you submit.
- **Score history** — past records, used to draw personal progress charts.
- **DM notification subscriptions** — only if you choose to subscribe; stores which player you want to be notified about.
- **Per-server configuration** — settings configured by server administrators (e.g. ranking channel, rank-role tiers, language).

## 2. Data we process but do NOT store

- **Screenshots** — when you submit a result, your screenshot is read by OCR to extract the boss name and score. The image is processed in memory and is **not** retained by the bot afterwards.
- **Message content** — the bot's optional chat assistant reads a message's text **only** when you directly @mention the bot or reply to one of its messages. All other messages are ignored. This text is used solely to generate a reply and is **not** stored, logged, or used for any other purpose.

## 3. Third-party services

To provide some features, limited data may be sent to the following processors:

- **Google (Gemini Vision)** — when AI-assisted OCR is enabled, the submitted screenshot is sent to Google's API to read the score.
- **Anthropic (Claude) or xAI (Grok)** — when you @mention or reply to the bot's chat assistant, the text of your message is sent to the configured AI provider to generate a reply.
- **Discord** — the platform the bot operates on.

We do **not** sell your data or share it with anyone other than the processors listed above.

## 4. Data retention

Stored scores and configuration are kept for as long as the bot is in use on your server. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Contact). If the bot is removed from a server, that server's configuration is no longer used.

## 5. Your rights

You may request to view or delete the data the bot holds about you. To do so, contact us using the details below.

## 6. Children's privacy

The bot is intended for Discord users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below that age.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.
